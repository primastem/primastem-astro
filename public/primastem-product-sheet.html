PrimaSTEM Product Sheet

Screen-free coding and mathematics · Ages 4–10 · Made from wood

PrimaSTEM is a wooden programmable robot that teaches both coding and mathematics without a screen. Children place NFC command tokens on a tangible control board to build programs they can see and touch.

Two curriculum levels in one device: logic for ages 4–7 and real mathematics for ages 7–10. The robot draws geometric figures — stars, spirals, polygons — making math visible.

CE & UKCA certified. Made in Cannes, France. Complete kit with online lesson plans in 10 languages.

What's in the kit

Ladybug Robot

  • Diameter 125 mm, height 44 mm
  • Wood
  • USB-C charging, 2–3 hours battery
  • Marker holder for drawing

Control Board

  • 317 × 217 mm, wood
  • 11 dual slots (6 main + 5 function)
  • Bluetooth ~5 m
  • Voice feedback speaker

Command Tokens

  • 100 pcs, 33 × 33 mm, cardboard, NFC 13.56 MHz
  • NFC 13.56 MHz
  • Scratch color coding
  • Writable via free phone app
